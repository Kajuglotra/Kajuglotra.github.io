About

Largest suppliers for the frozen Indian Snacks products.

  • 2017-2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In the year 2017, two Marathi Enterprenuers Rupesh and Rushikesh from the Wine capital of India 'Nashik' came together for there common vision in food industry. With the insight of the bright future of Food Industry in India they decided to stepup in the Frozen food Industry. With Rupesh having knowledge of food due to his Cafe Network (THE HANGOUT PLACE) in Nashik and Rushikesh trading network created a perfect team for this vision.They started there preparations with collecting the knowledge. By 2018, they started the Trading firm under DOAF name.

  • April, 2019

    An Agency is Born

    Starting with the small number of local customers in Pune and adjoining areas. With there determination and hardwork by the end of 2018 they had achieved business collaborations with top companies and some big franchise chains of India. The development and success they achieved in the trading made them tontake a next step in the industry to start there own Production plant with there own Brand Name "DOAF FOODS". And the story continues from there..

  • July 2019

    Our Missions

    To provide the best quality Indian food with the authentic taste to the customers through out the world.

  • July 2019

    Our Vissions

    “To become the benchmark manufacturing company for the frozen food industry providing world-class products to satisfied customers through continuous improvement driven by the integrity, teamwork and creativity of our people.”

Cooking Instruction

Just follow the simple steps, and get the best food by yourself.

  • Step-1

    STEP-1

    PreHeat Fryer to 190 °C

    Preheat your fryer at 190 °C OR 350 °F .

  • Step-2

    STEP-2

    Deep Fry your frozen Product

    remove Your Frozen Food from freezer and start deep frying your product for 5-7 minutes.

  • Step-3

    STEP-3

    Check it's golden crisp

    Fry your product until golden crisp, drain excess oil in a towel.

  • Step-4

    STEP-4

    Place Food on a sheet pan.

    After cooking it, It is ready to serve.
